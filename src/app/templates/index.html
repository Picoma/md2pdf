md2pdf

Conversion rapide de documents écrits en md vers du pdf. Facile à déployer pour une organisation.

Features

Simple

A workflow so simple, even my boss could understand it. Download a Markdown editor (any one !), download a template and get writing. Come back when you are done, and get your PDF for distribution !

Customisable

Your templates, your documents, your styles, your work flow. Whether are a geek curious about Markdown, want to save space by using lighter documents file, or a big corporation wanting to integrate an easy Markdown convertion tool to a bigger chain, this tool can fit your needs !

Integrable

Not only is this app designed to use a simple interface for human, it also implements a simple interface for machines as well. Tune the flow by hand, then automate it by intergationg it to your CI/CD pipeline.
